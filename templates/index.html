How to Use This Tool?

Welcome to our free suite of AI tools. Whether you are a content creator looking to download inspiration for your next YouTube thumbnail, or a designer needing a quick transparent image, we have you covered.

For YouTube Thumbnail Downloader:
  1. Go to YouTube and copy the link (URL) of the video.
  2. Paste the link in the input box above.
  3. Click on the "Get Thumbnails" button.
  4. Choose your preferred resolution (HD, SD, Normal) and click the Download button below the image.
For AI Background Remover:
  1. Switch to the "Background Remover" tab.
  2. Click "Choose File" and upload your image (JPG, PNG).
  3. Click "Remove Background" and wait a few seconds.
  4. Change background color if you want, and click download!
